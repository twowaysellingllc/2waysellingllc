2Wayselling LLC Website

Files included:
- index.html
- styles.css
- script.js

How to make it live:
1. Buy/register the domain: 2waysellingllc.com
2. Create a hosting account with Vercel, Netlify, Hostinger, GoDaddy, or similar.
3. Upload these files to your hosting account.
4. Point your domain DNS to the hosting provider.
5. Replace contact details in index.html:
   - Phone number
   - Email
   - Business address
6. Connect the booking form to email, Google Forms, Formspree, Calendly, or a backend.
